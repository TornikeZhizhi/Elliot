The Asia-Europe Foundation (ASEF) commissioned Moby Digg with the development and implementation of the photo exhibition On the Go, which will be presented in 30 countries. On the Go explores the cultural differences as well as similarities between Asia and Europe under the guidance of an international jury based in Singapore. With a total number of more then 1.500 photo submissions from 52 countries, the exhibition gained great international awareness and reach. The event was opened by the major of the city and the foreign minister of Luxemburg in November 2015. Moby Digg was in charge for the complete development, implementation and production of this international cultural project.
